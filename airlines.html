Airlines

Southwest Airlines
Southwest Airlines is a major American airline headquartered in Dallas, Texas. It is known for its low-cost fares and no-frills service, making air travel accessible to millions of passengers annually across the United States.
EgyptAir
EgyptAir is the flag carrier of Egypt, headquartered in Cairo. As a member of the Star Alliance, it operates scheduled passenger and cargo services to over 75 destinations in the Middle East, Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Americas.
Emirates
Emirates is one of the world's largest international airlines, based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Known for luxury and exceptional service, Emirates connects passengers to destinations across six continents with its modern fleet of aircraft.
Air Britain
Air Britain is a fictional airline name often used in aviation mockups and concepts. If referring to the historical aviation society, Air-Britain is a UK-based organization dedicated to the preservation and study of aviation history worldwide.
Lufthansa
Lufthansa is the largest German airline and one of the leading carriers in Europe. Headquartered in Frankfurt, it operates flights to destinations across the globe and is a founding member of the Star Alliance.
Air France
Air France is the flag carrier of France, headquartered in Paris. A major global airline, it operates extensive domestic and international services and is a founding member of the SkyTeam alliance.
Qatar Airways
Qatar Airways is the state-owned flag carrier of Qatar, based in Doha. Renowned for its luxury service and world-class airport, it serves over 170 destinations and has consistently ranked among the world’s top airlines.
Turkish Airlines
Turkish Airlines is the national flag carrier of Turkey, headquartered in Istanbul. It flies to more countries than any other airline in the world, offering extensive connectivity across Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas.
SAUDIA
SAUDIA, also known as Saudi Arabian Airlines, is the national carrier of Saudi Arabia. Headquartered in Jeddah, it connects the Kingdom to destinations across the Middle East, Europe, Asia, Africa, and North America.
Oman Air
Oman Air is the national airline of Oman, based in Muscat. Known for its premium service and modern fleet, it offers international flights to destinations across the Middle East, Asia, Africa, and Europe.
